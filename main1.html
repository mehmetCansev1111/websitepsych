Victim Blaming and Social Vindication of Offender: Victim Blaming in Domestic Violence Cases

In Australia, it was reported that domestic and sexual violence an estimate of 1.1 million adults have been victims of physical or sexual assault. A horrible crime as such would usually be met with a negative community reaction, however, the blame can often be projected at the victim.

Victim blaming is dangerous because it breeds a negative environment, marginalizing victims and making it difficult for victims to report these crimes. The purpose of this website is to bring attention to victim blaming, its effects and possible support systems and pathways to assist those affected
